## REAL CCTV PLACEMENT

In reality, each intersection has 4 cameras.
One camera per road ARM, mounted on a pole,
facing DOWN the road TOWARD the intersection.

So for intersection S7 "Market Square":
- CAM-S7-N: mounted on North arm, facing south (toward center)
- CAM-S7-S: mounted on South arm, facing north (toward center)  
- CAM-S7-E: mounted on East arm, facing west (toward center)
- CAM-S7-W: mounted on West arm, facing east (toward center)

Total CCTVs = intersections × 4
For 4 intersections = 16 CCTV feeds shown in grid

## WHAT EACH CCTV CAMERA ACTUALLY SEES

This is NOT a top-down view anymore.
This is a PERSPECTIVE view — like a real CCTV mounted 
on a pole 4-5 meters high, angled slightly downward,
looking straight down the road.

Think: you are standing on a footpath, 
camera is above you on a pole, 
looking down the road toward a traffic light ahead.

DRAW THIS ON CANVAS (perspective projection):

ROAD:
- Two lanes visible (one toward camera, one away — 
  but since camera faces intersection, 
  both lanes go AWAY from camera toward signal)
- Road drawn in perspective:
  - Near edge (bottom of canvas): wide (~80% canvas width)
  - Far edge (top-center): narrow (vanishing point)
  - Left kerb line converges to vanishing point
  - Right kerb line converges to vanishing point
  - Lane divider dashed line: converges to center
- Road color: #1c1c1c
- Lane markings: white dashed lines
- Stop line: solid white line near vanishing point

ENVIRONMENT:
- Sky at top 20% of canvas: very dark (#0d1117)
- Footpath/pavement strips on left and right edges
- Building facades on left and right (perspective boxes, 
  dark grey, a few lit windows #ffcc77)
- Streetlight poles on left side: tall thin line with 
  warm glow circle at top
- Trees: dark silhouettes on right footpath

TRAFFIC SIGNAL:
- Visible in distance near vanishing point
- Vertical pole with 3 signal lights
- Only one light active at a time (red/yellow/green)
- Glow effect on active light

DISTANCE MARKERS:
- Faint dashed lines across road every ~40px (perspective scaled)
- Simulates road marking distance indicators

## VEHICLES IN PERSPECTIVE VIEW

Vehicles appear as rectangles that:
- Spawn at the VANISHING POINT (far end, small)
- Move TOWARD the camera (grow larger as they approach)
- Exit at bottom of canvas (close to camera = large)

OR (opposite direction):
- Spawn at bottom of canvas (large)
- Move AWAY toward vanishing point (shrink)
- Exit at vanishing point

Perspective scaling formula:
  scale = (y_position / canvas_height)
  width = base_width * scale
  height = base_height * scale

Vehicle types (same as before):
- Sedan, SUV, Truck, Auto rickshaw
- Each in its own lane
- Vehicles going TOWARD intersection: right lane
- Vehicles going AWAY from intersection: left lane
- Vehicles STOP near stop line at RED signal
- Vehicles RESUME at GREEN signal

## AMBULANCE IN PERSPECTIVE CCTV

When ambulance appears on a specific camera feed:

VISUALLY:
- Ambulance spawns at vanishing point (small, far away)
- Grows larger as it approaches camera
- White body, red cross on roof
- Siren: two tiny rectangles on top alternating red/blue 
  every 300ms
- Moves faster than other vehicles
- Other vehicles near it slow down and pull aside
  (left lane vehicles drift further left)

DETECTION MOMENT:
- When ambulance reaches ~40% of canvas height 
  (clear enough to "detect"):
  - Flash white overlay on that canvas for 200ms
  - "⚠ AMBULANCE DETECTED" text appears center canvas
  - "CONF: 0.96" below it
  - Both fade out after 1.5s
  - Canvas gets red glowing border (box-shadow only)
  - Status badge → EMERGENCY (red, pulsing)
  - ALL other logic triggers (logs, route, corridor)
