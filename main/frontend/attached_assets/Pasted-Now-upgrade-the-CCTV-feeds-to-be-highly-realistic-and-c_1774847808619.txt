### CAMERA FEEL
Apply these CSS/canvas effects on every feed:

1. SCANLINE OVERLAY
   - Horizontal lines across entire feed
   - opacity: 0.08, spacing: 3px
   - Slight vertical scroll animation (moves down slowly)

2. NOISE / GRAIN
   - Random pixel noise redrawn every 3 frames
   - opacity: 0.04-0.06
   - Makes it feel like real CCTV sensor noise

3. VIGNETTE
   - Dark gradient from corners toward center
   - Simulates real camera lens darkening at edges

4. COLOR GRADE
   - Slight green tint (0.95 saturation, hue shift +10deg)
   - Old CCTV cameras have this greenish tinge
   - Apply via canvas filter: "saturate(0.9) hue-rotate(10deg)"

5. TIMESTAMP OVERLAY (bottom left of each feed)
   - Real live time: "2026-03-30  14:23:07"
   - Font: monospace, white, small (10px)
   - Slightly transparent background bar behind it

6. CCTV ID OVERLAY (top left)
   - "CAM-S1" style label
   - White monospace text with semi-transparent background

7. RECORDING INDICATOR (top right)
   - Blinking red circle + "REC" text
   - Blinks every 1 second

8. COMPRESSION ARTIFACTS (subtle)
   - Every 8-12 seconds, briefly show a horizontal glitch line
   - 1-2 pixel tall, wrong color, lasts 100ms
   - Simulates CCTV compression artifacts
